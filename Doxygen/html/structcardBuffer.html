Cardbuffer struct.

This struct will be for managing the checked in cards and managing the free spaces in the buffer A cardbuffer has : spaces_occupied = amount of spaces occupied by a checked in card checkinStation = place where the perticulair card has checked in checkins = UID's of the checked in chards available = array which indicates what index is free for a new card
